Study Destinations

Choose between two of the world's most advanced education systems and thriving economies.

Study in Germany

Germany offers world-class education with little to no tuition fees at public universities. It's an ideal destination for engineering, technology, and business studies.

  • Tuition-free education at public universities
  • 18 months post-study work permit
  • Strong economy with job opportunities
  • World-ranked universities
  • Part-time work allowed
Learn More About Germany

Study in Japan

Japan combines cutting-edge technology with rich cultural heritage. It offers excellent education in engineering, robotics, and business with scholarship opportunities.

  • High-quality education system
  • Various scholarship options available
  • Safe and technologically advanced society
  • Part-time work allowed
  • Pathway to employment after graduation
